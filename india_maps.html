Repo for Maps for India (Motivation)

As I was looking for Indian maps on the internet I realized that good Indian maps for cities, administrative boundaries etc are hard to find.

Details

Curently the repository has Administrative maps of India and ward level maps of Mumbai and Chennai. With a little bit of help, I could make this to be a definitive repository for Indian maps, or submit these maps to another better managed (and more complete) repository. In short this is just a start!
